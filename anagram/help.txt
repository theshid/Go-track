int charPos = word.charAt(0) - 'a';


It gives you the position relative to the a char in the character (ASCII) table. If the input char is lowercase, this is also the position in the alphabet (zero index based).

Example:

'Z' - 'a' gives you 90 - 97 = -7

'b' - 'a' gives you 98 - 97 = 1
